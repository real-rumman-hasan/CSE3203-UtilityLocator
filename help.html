Help & User Guide

This page explains how to use the Utility Locator platform. Whether you are looking for a service provider or offering services yourself, the following guide will help you understand how to use all the features of the website.

Creating an Account

To use the platform, you first need to create an account.

Steps to Register:

  1. Click the Register button on the homepage.
  2. Fill in the required information such as your name, email, contact details, and password.
  3. Choose whether you want to register as a Customer or a Service Provider.
  4. Submit the registration form.
  5. After successful registration, you can log in using your credentials.

If you already have an account, simply click the Login button and enter your email and password.

For Customers

Customers are users who want to hire service providers for different types of utility work.

Finding a Service Provider

  1. After logging in, you will be directed to the homepage.
  2. The homepage displays different service categories (such as electrical, gas, sanitary, locksmith, shifting, etc.) in the form of cards.
  3. Click on the service card that matches the service you need.
  4. A list of available service providers for that category will appear.
  5. Select the provider you want to hire.

Viewing a Provider Profile

After selecting a provider, you will be taken to the provider’s profile page.

Here you can:

  1. View information about the provider.
  2. Start a chat with the provider to discuss the job.
  3. Send a job request to hire the provider.

Sending a Job Request

To request a service:

  1. Go to the provider’s profile page.
  2. Click the Request Job button.
  3. Provide any necessary details about the job.
  4. Submit the request.

The provider will receive your request and can accept or reject it.

Checking Your Requested Jobs

Customers can track their requests easily.

  1. Go to your profile page.
  2. View the list of pending or accepted job requests.
  3. You can also chat with the provider from there if needed.

For Service Providers

Providers are users who offer services to customers through the platform.

Receiving Job Requests

You can see the requests sent to you by:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Go to your profile page.
  3. You will see a list of job requests sent by customers.

Accepting or Rejecting Requests

For each request you receive:

  1. Review the job details.
  2. You may chat with the customer to clarify the job requirements.
  3. Choose to Accept or Reject the job request.

Once accepted, the job will move to your active job list.

Additional Features

Chat System

Both customers and providers can communicate through the built-in chat system to discuss job details before accepting or completing a job.

Job History

All users can view their past work.

  1. Customers can see the services they have requested.
  2. Providers can see the jobs they have completed.

This information is available in the profile page under job history.
